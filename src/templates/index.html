Carpicker

In this webapplication, you can choose which energylabel you want for your car. All types of cars with that specific energy label will show up. The cars will be sorted on the fuel usage per 100 km. Do you prefer to choose your car first. No problem, you can also choose your type of car and the associated label will be given.
